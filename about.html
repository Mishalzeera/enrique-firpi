Enrique Firpi is one of the keystone musicians in the international Latin Jazz scene. He has played with Sergio Mendes, Ivan Lins, Verdine White as well as countless respected musicians around the world. Born in Uruguay in 1960, he started playing drums at the age of nine. At sixteen, he started his life-long career as a professional musician.

In 1989 Enrique moved to the Netherlands with his wife, singer Beatriz Aguiar. Since then he has played with an exhaustive list of expat Latin and jazz talent. He counts many noteable musicians as his close friends, and has toured extensively throughout Europe, Asia and the Americas.

Enrique has performed with: Laura Fygi, New Conrad Miller Trio, Leonardo Amuedo, Lilian Vieira, Dori Caymmi, Vera Mann, Nueva Manteca, Izaline Calister, Deborah J Carter, Randal Corsen, Sergio Mendes, Ramon Valle and Orlando ¨Maraca¨ Valle, Tico Pierhagen Aguabajo and Kris Berry just to name a few.

His recording credits include: New Conrad Miller Trio, Leonardo Amuedo, Josee Koning, Deborah J Carter, Laura Fygi, Vera Mann, Jose Lopretti, Esther Steenbergen among many others.
