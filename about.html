Enrique Firpi is one of the keystone musicians in the international Latin Jazz scene. He has played with Sergio Mendes, Ivan Lins, Verdine White as well as countless respected musicians around the world. Born in Uruguay in 1960, he taught himself drums at the age of nine. At sixteen, he started his life-long career as a professional musician.

In 1989 Enrique moved to the Netherlands with his wife, singer Beatriz Aguilar. Since then he has played with an exhaustive list of expat Latin and jazz talent. He counts many noteable musicians as his close friends, and has toured extensively throughout Europe, Asia and the Americas.

Enrique has taught workshops and masterclasses at the conservatories of Amsterdam, Rotterdam, Utrect, Arnhem and Groningen.


He is currently teaching committed private students, see the contact page if interested to apply.

Enrique has performed with: Laura Fygi, New Conrad Miller trio, Leonardo Amuedo, Lilian Vieira, Dori Caymmi, Vera Mann, Nueva Manteca, Izaline Calister, Deborah J Carter, Randal Corsen, Sergio Mendes, Ramon Valle and Orlando ¨Maraca¨ Valle, Tico Pierhagen Aguabajo and Kris Berry just to name a few.

His recording credits include: Leonardo Amuedo Otro Mundo, Josee Koning Dois mundos, Deborah J Carter Round Moonlight, Girl talking and Daytripper, Laura Fygi Latin Touch, Vera Mann Diana, Jose Lopretti Candombe, Esther Steenbergen Repertoire, Dirindi Cantar do Jobim, among many others.
